Our Cookie Philosophy

At DevTools Hub, we believe in minimal, transparent, and privacy-respecting cookie usage. We use only essential cookies that improve your experience without tracking you across the internet.

🍪 Key Point: We use only functional cookies. No tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, no third-party marketing cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Cookies We Use

Here's a complete list of cookies we use on DevTools Hub:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type

theme_preference Saves your preferred color theme 7 days Functional
cookie_consent Records that you've seen our cookie notice 30 days Essential

No Tracking or Advertising Cookies

We do not use:

  • ❌ Google Analytics cookies
  • ❌ Facebook/Meta pixels
  • ❌ Advertising cookies of any kind
  • ❌ Third-party tracking cookies
  • ❌ Cross-site tracking cookies

Third-Party Cookies

Since our site is hosted on GitHub Pages, GitHub may set their own essential cookies for security and functionality. These are governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect some functionality of our tools.

Cookie Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of essential cookies as described in this policy. We do not use any non-essential cookies that require explicit consent.

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top.
