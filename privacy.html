Our Commitment to Privacy

At DevTools Hub, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we handle your information when you use our tools and services.

🔐 Key Point: All our tools run entirely in your browser. We never send your data to any server. What you type stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We collect zero personal data through our tools. Since all processing happens locally in your browser, we have no access to:

  • The text you enter in any tool
  • The passwords you generate
  • The JSON you format
  • The Base64 you encode or decode
  • Any files you process

Automatic Anonymous Data

Like most websites, we may collect anonymous usage statistics through GitHub Pages:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • General geographic region (country level only)

This information is completely anonymous and cannot be linked to you personally.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies essential for website functionality:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Preference cookies: Remember your tool settings (like password length) during your visit

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party marketing cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic access logs as described in their Privacy Statement.

Children's Privacy

Our services are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your data, there's nothing to secure on our end. Your privacy is protected by design:

  • ✅ All tools run locally in your browser
  • ✅ No data transmission to any server
  • ✅ No databases or user accounts
  • ✅ No tracking or analytics scripts

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
